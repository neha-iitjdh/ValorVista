About ValorVista

AI-powered real estate valuation platform built with modern machine learning techniques.

Our Mission

ValorVista empowers buyers, sellers, and real estate professionals with instant, accurate property valuations powered by advanced machine learning.

The Technology

ValorVista uses a Gradient Boosting Regressor, one of the most powerful algorithms for structured data prediction. Here's what makes our model effective:

  • Ensemble Learning: Combines 500 decision trees for robust predictions
  • Feature Engineering: Creates 20+ derived features including quality-age interactions
  • Log Transformation: Handles the right-skewed nature of home prices
  • Cross-Validation: Validated using 5-fold CV to prevent overfitting
  • Uncertainty Estimation: Provides confidence intervals using staged predictions

Data Source

Our model is trained on the Ames Housing Dataset, a comprehensive collection of 1,460 residential property sales in Ames, Iowa. The dataset includes 79 features covering:
