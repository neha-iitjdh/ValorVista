Key Insights
Living Area Matters Most

Above-ground living area is the strongest predictor of home value, contributing approximately 15% to the model's decision.

Quality Over Size

Overall quality rating has a significant impact. A 1-point increase in quality can add 5-10% to home value.

Location Premium

Premium neighborhoods like NridgHt and NoRidge command 40-60% higher prices than average areas.

Age Impact

Newer homes typically command premium prices, but well-maintained older homes can still achieve strong valuations.

Basement Value

Finished basement space adds value, but at a lower rate per sq ft than above-ground living space.

Garage Capacity

Two-car garages are standard. Three-car garages typically indicate higher-end properties.
